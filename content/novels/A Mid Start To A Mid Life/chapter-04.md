As expected from an average person, my cultivation has been average.

I have improved several of my meridian nodes. I have added new layers to my chi string, and the flow has been going smoothly. Nothing spectacular. Nothing worth writing a legend about.

Average, as I said.

I did hear some news, though.

One of the senior brothers from our peak managed to get himself a fortuitous encounter. Apparently, he found it in some strange valley located near the sect’s outer perimeter.

Like every cliché cultivation sect, ours sends disciples there every once in a while to temper themselves against demonic beasts.

The sect maintains the beasts' strength at a convenient level. Not too strong, not too weak. Just enough to give disciples some real combat experience without turning them into food.

At least, that’s what I assume they do. Nobody really explains these things to ordinary disciples.

Anyway, that senior brother somehow discovered a fortuitous encounter there. With the power of plot armor—sorry, I mean luck—he managed to break through his bottleneck.

He was incredibly happy. Apparently, he had been stuck there for who knows how long.

So naturally, he decided to throw a celebration party.

And he invited me.

Well, technically, he invited people from my peak. The others from my little group weren't exactly close enough to be considered his friends, so I ended up being one of the attendees.

I went to the party.

And just like every cultivation novel, the script played itself out.

You congratulate the person who broke through.

You give them a gift.

You say something like:

"Brother, remember me when you reach the Foundation Establishment Realm."

"You have great potential."

"One day, you will surely rise above us."

And so on.

The usual stuff.

Honestly, nobody believes half of what they say, but that's just how these things work.

However, this time, something new happened.

A problem appeared.

A cliché problem.

I noticed another disciple from a different peak. Specifically, the War Peak.

It had been a while since I last saw someone from that place. Those guys were usually either training or doing missions outside the sect.

You don't ask what they're doing.

They don't tell you.

Everyone understands the arrangement.

Anyway, this disciple happened to have a conflict with someone else.

The reason?

Of course.

They both liked the same woman.

Because apparently, even in a cultivation sect, people cannot escape the ancient tradition of creating unnecessary drama over romance.

And just like every cliché, it quickly turned into pointless arguments, stubborn pride, and the kind of resentment that usually leads to future schemes.

Everyone immediately tried to calm them down.

Because despite what novels and audiences might think, real people don't enjoy watching two cultivators destroy a perfectly good party.

You don't want your celebration to turn into a battlefield.

Fortunately, everyone managed to keep both sides under control.

Unfortunately, our party host was not happy.

He was already annoyed enough that he indirectly asked everyone to leave.

And, well...

The party ended badly.

Still, I gave him my gift.

That should count for something.

Hopefully, in the future, he won't decide to bully me because of this.

Not that I think he would.

People here are actually quite reasonable.

But...

Better safe than sorry.
